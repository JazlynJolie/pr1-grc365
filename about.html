About the Brand

Jolei (JaeJolei) was made with the intention to bring effortless beauty and confidence to anyone, no matter where life takes them. Based in las vegas, Jolei creates hand-made press-on nails with love and attention to detail. Each set is designed to offer a flawless, custom fit that’s both durable and comfortable. Using high-quality materials, Jolei provide a beautiful finish that’s made to last. Whether you're after bold, on-trend designs or timeless elegance, Jolei empowers you to express your unique personality while making a positive impact on the planet.

Founded by Jazlyn Jolie, Jolei started off as a hobby Jazlyn explored in october 2023. after making sets for friends and family, she began selling her first press-ons in december 2023.
